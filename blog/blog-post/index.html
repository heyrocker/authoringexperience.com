Welcome to Authoring Experience

January 19, 2025 by Greg Dunlap

Hello and welcome to the new site authoringexperience.com. On its face, this is a site for my new book Designing Content Authoring Experiences. I will probably be making some posts about the book, how it came to be, the process of self-publishing it, and topics related to the CMS space in general. However, for some time I have also wanted a place to be putting thoughts about trends in the industry, thorny problems I’m working on, and self-reflections about general topics I care about. These could be big or small, broad or very focused. Hey I have thoughts about modern page builders! I also have thoughts about what widget to use in select lists. I also have thoughts about Survivor and navigating a world that seems bent on beating us down inch by inch, day by day, and year by year.

I may also never write another post and this will be all you ever see here. Time will tell.

I’d love to hear from you too! What do you want to hear about? What burning questions do you have? Hit me up and I’ll do my best to talk about it.
